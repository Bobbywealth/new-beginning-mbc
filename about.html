Our Story

About
Our
Church

New Beginning Missionary Baptist Church is more than a building — we're a community on mission to love God, love people, and change our world.

A Place
For You

For over two decades, New Beginning has been a spiritual home for families across Atlanta. What started as a small gathering of believers has grown into a thriving, multi-generational church rooted in faith, fellowship, and service.

Our doors are open to everyone — no matter where you are in your journey. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a deeper community, there's a place for you here.

Pastor Eric Reid
SENIOR PASTOR
